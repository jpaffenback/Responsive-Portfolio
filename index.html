About Me

My name is Jason Paffenback, an Atlanta Georgia native with a very unique last name. I graduated from Georgia State University with degree in Economics and minor in Sociology. I landed my first real job in banking right after graduating. Here's a fun fact, the company is located only a couple miles down the road from the hospital where I was born.

After 8+ years in my current position I have realized I am ready for a change and new challenge. I am back in school (Georgia Institute of Technology more commonly referred to as Georgia Tech) learning new skill sets to compliment my past experience in opening new doors for me. I want to be challenged to learn new things and create really cool stuff along the way. I want to be free of the 9-5 rat-race commonly found in more traditional feilds such as banking.
